Detailed Project Analysis

Cognitive Load

Trying to lip-read or read static text on a phone while listening is mentally exhausting. AirScript reduces this load by placing text in the line of sight.

üëÅÔ∏è

Visual Focus

Current apps force users to look down at screens ("Heads-Down"). AirScript enables "Heads-Up" communication, maintaining eye contact.

üé≠

Emotional Context

Standard captions lack tone. Our NLP engine analyzes sentiment, making angry text shaky/red and happy text glowing/green.

Feasibility Strategy

  • Latency: Utilizing on-device lightweight models (Vosk) for immediate feedback, refined by Cloud API.
  • Hardware: Compatible with standard ARCore/ARKit phones; no expensive glasses required initially.

Challenges & Risks

  • Noise Interference: Background noise reduces STT accuracy. Mitigation: Noise suppression algorithms.
  • Battery Drain: AR + AI is power intensive. Mitigation: Optimization of frame rendering rates.
